How to Test

  1. Create a Meeting: Click "Create Meeting" to generate a new meeting room
  2. Join as Host: You'll automatically enter the meeting room as the host
  3. Test Controls: Try muting/unmuting, turning camera on/off, and screen sharing
  4. Demo Participants: The app will simulate additional participants joining
  5. Explore Features: Check out the chat, participants sidebar, and responsive design
